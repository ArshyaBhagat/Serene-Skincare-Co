  • Our Story

    Skin Co. was founded on the belief that everyone deserves access to effective, trustworthy skincare information without the burden of high costs or overwhelming complexity. The idea was born when its founders, Arshya Bhagat and Srushti Tonde, met in college and bonded over their shared struggles with skincare. Both had spent years navigating an endless cycle of trial and error, searching for solutions to their unique skin concerns. These experiences not only shaped their personal journeys but also inspired a greater mission. Through countless conversations, shared recommendations, and experiments, Arshya and Srushti recognized that a widespread problem—reliable and affordable skincare advice was hard to find. The industry often felt exclusive, with complex jargon, conflicting information, and marketing designed to sell rather than educate. Realizing this gap, they set out to create a solution that would empower others to take control of their skincare journeys. Thus, Serene Skin Co. was born—a platform offering free, precise, and evidence-based skincare information for everyone. Whether addressing common concerns like acne, sensitivity, or dryness, or helping people understand active ingredients and how to build a routine, the platform quickly became a trusted resource. Every article and guide was designed to make skincare less intimidating and more accessible, ensuring that everyone, regardless of background or experience, could find value. Serene Skin Co. isn’t just about sharing skincare tips—it’s about creating a movement of empowerment. The platform aims to demystify skincare, debunk myths, and enable people to make informed decisions about their skin without feeling pressured to spend on expensive products or treatments. Arshya and Srushti’s commitment to authenticity and transparency lies at the heart of every piece of content they create. As the community around Serene Skin Co. has grown, so too has the founders’ vision. The positive feedback and encouragement from users have inspired Arshya and Srushti to dream bigger. They now plan to expand their mission beyond providing information to creating their own line of eco-friendly skincare products in the future. These products will reflect the same values that define Serene Skin Co.—simplicity, authenticity, and effectiveness. Arshya and Srushti are committed to ensuring that these products are crafted with care, using high-quality, sustainably sourced ingredients. They also plan to prioritize environmentally friendly practices, such as biodegradable packaging and cruelty-free formulations. By keeping sustainability at the core, they aim to contribute not only to healthier skin but also to a healthier planet. While the product line is still in the early stages of planning, the vision is clear: to create a range of skincare solutions that are as effective as they are ethical. Arshya and Srushti are taking the time to research, test, and develop formulations that truly meet the needs of their community while staying true to their commitment to sustainability. Today, Serene Skin Co. remains a trusted platform, empowering people to navigate their skincare journeys with confidence. The future is bright, with plans to introduce products that will complement the knowledge and guidance the platform already provides. Serene Skin Co. is more than just a brand—it’s a movement. With their community by their side, Arshya and Srushti are excited to shape the future of skincare, making it accessible, transparent, and sustainable for everyone.
